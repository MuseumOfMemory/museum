Family of a disappeared person

Carmen Teresa Navas

Mother of Víctor Hugo Quero Navas. Still waiting to be told the truth.

Carmen Teresa Navas is 82 years old. Her son Víctor Hugo Quero Navas was detained on January 1, 2025 and transferred to Tocorón Prison. The family reports that since then he has been forcibly disappeared — they have never been given official information about his whereabouts. According to information that has reached them, Víctor Hugo died on July 27, 2025. His death has not been officially confirmed. What remains for Carmen is the absence of truth.

What is known

What we know, and what we do not

Carmen Teresa Navas is the mother of Víctor Hugo Quero Navas. On January 1, 2025, her son was detained and transferred to Tocorón Prison. From the moment of that transfer, his family has not been given official information about where he is, how he is, or whether he is alive.

On July 27, 2025, Carmen received word — through unofficial channels — that Víctor Hugo had died. That word has not been confirmed by any authority. No body has been delivered. No certificate has been issued. No explanation has been given. For the family, this is not the end of a story. It is the absence of one.

The right to know

Under international human rights law, families of the disappeared have the right to know the truth: where their loved one is held, what condition they are in, and — if they are no longer alive — when, where, and how they died, and where their remains rest. Carmen Teresa Navas has not been given any of this.

This profile exists because she has asked for it to exist. It will be updated the moment any official information is delivered. Until then, the record will stand as it is — incomplete, because so is the truth.

Sources & Method

How this profile was built

This profile is being built from the family's own report. As of this draft, the only confirmed source is direct family testimony provided to the Museum of Memory. We do not publish unverified claims as fact. Where information has not been confirmed by an official source, we say so plainly.

  1. Confirmed by the family: Carmen Teresa Navas is 82 years old. Her son Víctor Hugo Quero Navas was detained on January 1, 2025, and transferred to Tocorón Prison.
  2. Reported by the family: Since the transfer, no official information has been provided about Víctor Hugo's whereabouts. They report him as forcibly disappeared.
  3. Reported, unconfirmed: On July 27, 2025, the family received word that Víctor Hugo had died. No authority has confirmed this. No remains have been delivered.
  4. Pending: Recorded testimony from Carmen, on her terms and with her consent.
  5. Pending: Independent corroboration from monitoring organizations (e.g. Foro Penal, CEPAZ).
  6. Pending: Any official communication from authorities regarding Víctor Hugo's whereabouts or status.

Photographs are placeholders. We do not publish images of family members or of the disappeared without explicit consent from the family. This page will be updated the moment any new information — official or otherwise — becomes available.
